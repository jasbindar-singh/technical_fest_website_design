About Us

EXORSA is the Online Annual Technical Fest of MYWBUT.COM, the leading education portal of West Bengal. In the sixth year of EXORSA, students from WBUT (MAKAUT) affiliated colleges as well as students from other Universities will have the opportunity to show their talent among thousands of students. With more interesting events and more number of Prizes, EXORSA v6.0 is going to be one of the largest Online Technical Festival. As all the Events under EXORSA v6.0 are Online, students can participate from anywhere, no need to visit any particular place. It just requires Internet enabled computer and all done.
